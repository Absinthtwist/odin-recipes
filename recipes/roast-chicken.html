Roast Chicken

back to list

Here is a great recipe for home cooked chicken.

Ingredients

  • whole chicken
  • salt and pepper
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • onions

Directions

  1. chop potatoes, onion, and carrorts
  2. line bottom of large cook pan
  3. butter surface of chicken. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 450 degree oven for one hour.
