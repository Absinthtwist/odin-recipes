Lasagna

Back to the list

Everyone loves a good lasagna.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta
  • italian sausage
  • tomato sauce
  • onions

Directions

  1. parboil noodles
  2. saute sausage
  3. layer pasta, cheese, sauce
  4. cover with mozzarella
  5. bake 450 for an hour
